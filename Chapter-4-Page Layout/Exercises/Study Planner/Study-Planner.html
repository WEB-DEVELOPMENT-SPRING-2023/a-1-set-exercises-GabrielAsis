Module Overview

The Web Development module is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the core concepts and principles of web development. Students will learn basic and intermediate concepets of HTML, CSS, and Javascript. This module will provide 2 final projects for the students to complete that will prove their skills and knowledge on the topic. Learning web development is essential because it offers a wide range of career opportunities, enables individuals to build their websites and applications, provides a better understanding of technology, facilitates collaboration with designers, and offers cost-effective solutions for ongoing website maintenance and updates. In today's digital age, having a basic knowledge of web development can be incredibly beneficial, whether for personal or professional purposes.
