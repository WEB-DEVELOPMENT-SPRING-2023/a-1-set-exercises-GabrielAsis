Key Information

Breaking Bad is an American television drama series that aired on AMC from 2008 to 2013. The show was created, directed, and produced by Vince Gilligan who was known for writing and producing The X-Files. The show won multiple Oscar Awards and after its success Vince Gilligan created a spin-off show based on Breaking Bad titled "Better Call Saul".
