Plot

The Oscar winning show "Breaking Bad" is set in Albuquerque, New Mexico, between 2008 and 2010, and follows Walter White, a extremely overqualified high school chemistry teacher who transforms into a ruthless kingpin in the local methamphetamine drug business after being diagnosed with inoperable lung cancer and partnering up with his partner Jesse Pinkman.

Key Information

You can watch Breaking Bad on most streaming services like Netflix, Redbox, Vudu, Apple TV or Prime Video. The show is age-rated for 13+ yrs and includes 5 seasons and each season has around 13 episodes (except for the 1st and 5th season) lasting for almost an hour.
