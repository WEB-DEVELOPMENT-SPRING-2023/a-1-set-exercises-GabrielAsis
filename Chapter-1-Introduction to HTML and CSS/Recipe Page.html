Filipino Chicken Adobo Recepe

Chicken Adobo is a type of chicken stew from the Philippines. Chicken is marinated in soy sauce and spices before being pan-fried and stewed until tender. The dish became popular due to its delicious taste and ease of preparation.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken cut into serving pieces
  • 3 pieces of dried bay leaves
  • 4 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 8 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 5 cloves garlic crushed
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt optional
  • 1 teaspoon whole peppercorn
